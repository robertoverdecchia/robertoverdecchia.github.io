Hi there! I am Roberto Verdecchia, a Tenure Track Assistant Professor at the STLAB of the University of Florence.

My research interest lies in the intersection between Software Architecture and Technical Debt (which combined can look something like this).
I am also fascinated by topics related to Software Sustainability, Software Testing, and Software Education.

Prior to joining the STLAB, I was a Postdoctoral Research Associate of the S2Group at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam.

I hold a Double Ph.D. obtained at the Computer Science Department of the Gran Sasso Science Institute and at the S2Group of the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam under the supervision of Patricia Lago and Ivano Malavolta.
